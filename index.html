As a Solutions Architect, I am responsible with the design of the applications and services that the customer needs and wants. I make sure that the development team knows the direction of the application, what they will deliver, and that the applications will cater the throughput requirement when users are already using the applications and services by providing them insights on how to develop highly available and high throughput code.

Before being a solutions architect, I am also a Software Engineer with passion; Started from a humble beginning as a Junior Software Developer, thriving for success and aiming for the best output there can be in an application.

I have developed many types of programs from small tools to large enterprise applications and even desktop applications using Java Swing and .NET frameworks. It was not easy, but it was really worth it to develop different kinds of software in different platforms. I mainly focus on microservices, asynchronous processing, high throughput, and data stream processing.

Besides being a Software Engineer by profession, playing games is my hobby and also to relieve stress.
